Welcome to Priority AI

Priority AI keeps your inbox under control and turns any web page into an on-device AI workspace. Everything runs locally through Chrome's built-in models (Prompt, Summarizer, Translator, and Language Detector), so no content ever leaves your browser.

Getting started with Gmail

  1. Open gmail.com in a new tab.
  2. Click the Priority AI extension icon in your toolbar.
  3. Choose Enable AI Classification to allow Chrome to download the Gemini Nano model.
  4. Watch priority badges (Urgent / Action / FYI / Low) appear beside each conversation once the model is ready.

Use the floating workspace anywhere

  1. Press Alt + Shift + P (or click the floating bubble) to open the launcher.
  2. Select text or drag to capture an area. Priority AI auto-detects the language and shows the selection in a modal.
  3. Choose Summarize for a streaming TL;DR, or tap Translate to pick/search any destination language and watch the translation appear live.

Pro tips:

  • If you don't see "Built-in AI active," make sure you're running Chrome Dev/Canary with the Prompt/Summarizer/Translator flags turned on, then retry from the popup.
  • The launcher remembers where you leave it; drag it to a comfortable spot or hide/show it with Alt + Shift + P.
